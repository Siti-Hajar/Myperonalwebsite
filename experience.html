Be a Temporary Teacher


After I complete my studies from STPM, I have long holiday that I did not know what are thing that I have to do at home. So, I take this chance to improve my skills by be a temporary teacher. I be a teacher at “Sekolah Rendah Agama Al – Itqan” where nearest from my house. I get contract for one month to teach my students at the school. When I am teaching in this school, most of teachers, students as well as the staff always called me “Muallimah Hajar” where the word of “Muallimah” is stands for teacher.







Teachers, the staff and students are very nice to me. I am enjoying seeing many student behaviour when I was at school even I am teaching in front of class. However, I am very enjoy with them in teaching and give some storytelling to attract them to be interested in learning subject. Even, I have short time to teach them, I will remember these happy moment ever in my life. It is such memorable for me to have magical experience that I never had before. I am really love children. I hope I can have chance to teach and meet them again in future. Insya- Allah.
