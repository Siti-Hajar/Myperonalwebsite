My Small Family



This is my family. My family is not bigger as other family out there. After my parents divorced when I was 7 years old. Now, I am staying with my beloved mother who names, Che Maneh Binti Sheerat. I lost connection with my father who names, Rosli bin Raheen because after both of them been divorced, I cannot detect where my father is now be. My mother have six siblings, but all of them were died except her sister, Che Mariam binti Sheerat. Now, my mother and I live at Teluk Kumbar, Bayan Lepas, Penang. We stayed with my mother’s sister who is my aunty names Che Mariam binti Sheerat at my grandparent’s house. Because of I am the only child in my family. No more people that I can show of except to my mother and my aunt.



My Mother



So, let get to know about my mother. My mother names Che Maneh binti Sheerat. She is 58 years old. She now works as a babysitter at home full time. My mother has disease issues where she already has asthma and high blood pressure disease. She is be a single mother after has divorced with my father since 2007. From her salary as babysitter, it is enough for us especially for me to cover all my studies need when I was school until today. My mother is hardworking and motivational woman. She always giving me advice every day to keep being a better person and respect to other people who are older than me. Even, she has no higher level education like me, but every words that she says and experience that she go through along her life makes me also motivated to follow her step to be wonderful woman with many skills and brilliant mind in future.

My Aunty

After, my mother and I moved to Teluk Kumbar, now we can accompany her together. Previously, she has had worked at factory for almost 30 year experience. After, she feel tired to continue her working. She stay at home full time, and at the same time, it added with problem a variety of disease that make her forced to face like cholesterol disease and high blood pressure.



Therefore, I am as the only child in my family, should help and be take care of both of them because they are very old and hard to do anything that can give them hard pressure. However, I am very love them so much because they are my family that I ever have.
