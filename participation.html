Form 6 Student Representative Council in 2019


For my participation, I participate in many events and competition. But, in this website I just listed you a few of them. Firstly, I have been a person in charge of the funds of my Form 6 Student Representative Council in 2019. I am in charge for one year and it is my first time hold this position when I was at form 6. It is pretty much give me feel tricky to hold this responsibility, but with support from teachers and my friends, I can do this job well.





Go Green To Tanjong Bungah

As a person who has position in this body of form 6. My friends and I was make a trip to Tanjong Bungah in Balik Pulau area for make deal to build a farm for our school. This is our initiative as a plan to make our school be one of school who has natural environment in every school area. Alhamdulillah, this project was done successfully and every teachers also head master of the school very proud of my group body of form 6 session of 2019 because this achievement.





Poem Competition




Lastly is my participation in poem as I represent my school to state level of Penang. During this events, my friend and I have to recite a poem on the stage. Our poem title is title of “Green” who written by our own literature teacher names Madam Norly. After waiting for our turn to get the prize, however, our school lost. We be in 10th place out of 30 schools that competed. Although, we did not come back with prize or medals for school, but the moments when we go compete together is very memorable to me and my friends on that time.
